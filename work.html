SPY Analysis

This Python notebook analyzes the past 5 years of SPY data. It attempts to find trends and patterns in SPY daily changes. It attempts to find the optimal range of a SPY daily change that would be most often followed by a positive, green day.

This was a project I created on my own, inspired by my passion for investing. I hope to do more stock market or similar projects in the future.
